Exhibitions

Exhibition is a platform to acquaint students with some of the contemporary technologies engineered by scientific and technical honchos. TechSpardha boasts of its association with several institutes of scientific research, as well as technological firms that display their finest technologies, crafter by their finest engineering technologies. The exhibitions are also graced by academicians, and are ardently attended by the faculty and the staff during the span of the fest.

Wall-E is a kind of inspirational robot which is mainly designed to ensure that people instantly feel connected with even a lifeless bot and co-exist together in their everyday lives. It achieved fame in Pixar's animated movie, WALL-E. Special mention about the students of BARC University, Bangladesh for bringing to life an Animated Character to function exactly as it has been portrayed.

Google Glass is a headset, or optical head-mounted display, that is worn like a pair of eyeglasses. It helps you to interact with the Internet and the in a more obvious and physical manner. The Glass displays Information in a smart phone-like interface. With development going on in Google Glass Applications, the sky is the limit for its use in reality.

Gusto-Talks

Lecture Series allow students to interact with the experienced and successful personalities. Guest Lectures are given by people from different professions and on different topics ranging from entrepreneurship, technical to social responsibilities. This time, a lot of personalities will be giving lectures.

Guest Lectures are an integral part of Techspardha. Cooperate head honchos and people from academia are invited to share their spectra of rich experiences and insights about the contemporary technology and management relevant horizons. These guest lectures serve as an ideal platform for the students to supplement their knowledge with the first hand perspective of some of the stalwarts of the industry, and also appreciate the differnt dimensions of handling the challenges that lie ahead. Startup Fair

Introduction

StartUp Fair is one of the Biggest National Platform for Young Entrepreneurs and Interested Students being organized at Techspardha, NIT Kurukshetra in association with YOURSTORY. It features a variety of events including Presentations, Internship Offerings and Panel discussions with the Founders. Start-ups plan to interact and evaluate students based on speed interview and select students to provide them with a real start-up working experience.

Vision

Our goal is to introduce emerging StartUp Entrepreneurs with Interested Students who can add on to the Startup Atmosphere in India. We students are very eager to work with entrepreneurs to solve emerging business challenges.

What’s In It For Students?

  • Students get to discuss any questions regarding startup culture.
  • They also get to meet founders and attend the discussion round of all the pioneers of the industry.
  • Students stand a chance to get Internships from the Emerging StartUps

What’s In It For Start-Ups?

  • Start-Ups get a chance to connect with the students appropriate for the company they have built.
  • Start-Ups get to interact with each other and advertise to students and potential users during the fest/fair.
  • They also get to be interact with Mr. Deepak Goel, Advisor to Start-Ups.

Events And Activities?

  • Startup Intern Fair : Interviews and Written Rounds
  • Interaction round between Students and Start-Ups :
    • Founders Meet
    • Idea Presentation
  • Session with Mr. Deepak Goel (Ted Speaker Advisor/Investor of 500+ start-ups)
